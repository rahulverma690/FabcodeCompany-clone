Why are aligners the perfect alternative to braces?

Invisible aligners are comfortable to wear and allow gentle and precise tooth movement. They can be inconspicuously integrated into everyday life and show visible results after only 30 days.

Try it!

in three simple steps

Step 1

Send us impressions of your teeth

Order our dental impression set directly to your home. It contains everything you need for your impressions. Use our easy-to-follow instructions or watch our video.

Step 2

Our doctors create your 3D treatment plan

Using a 3D simulation, your individual treatment plan shows step-by-step how your teeth will shift and what the expected result will look like.

Step 3

We send your aligners to your home

Every 10 days you change to the next aligners. Our set also includes a retainer. You wear this at night so that your teeth always remain perfect after treatment.
